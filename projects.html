Projects

3D-Printed Arm Prostheses for LMICs

Designed affordable, functional arm prostheses for low- and middle-income countries (LMICs). Using 3D printing technology and affordable thermoplastics like polypropylene, the prostheses offer a customizable solution to individuals with arm amputations.

Laparoscopic Probe for Prostate Cancer Margin Detection

Developed the mechanical housing for a laparoscopic bioimpedance probe designed to detect positive surgical margins in prostate cancer patients during laparoscopic prostatectomy.

Gold Nanoparticle-Enhanced Radiation Therapy for Cancer

Developed a novel treatment for pancreatic cancer using gold nanoparticles functionalized with P1G4 antibodies. The aim was to assess the effectiveness of these nanoparticles in enhancing radiation therapy, targeting pancreatic tumors more specifically and improving treatment outcomes.

ENGS 33 Bridge Design Project

Designed a truss bridge as part of a solid mechanics course to test structural efficiency and material usage.

ENGS 31 UART Calculator Project

This project involved the development of a UART-based calculator using a Basys 3 board and a PS/2 keyboard.

Point-of-Care Cervical Cancer HPV Screening Device

Developed a low-cost, point-of-care device aimed at improving cervical cancer screening, particularly in low-resource settings such as Honduras.

Bottles, Bins, and Beyond (Recycling Contamination Reduction)

Developed a recycling bin system aimed at reducing contamination in beverage container recycling.

Sweat-Based Glucose Monitor

Developed a non-invasive glucose monitoring device that uses sweat as a medium to measure glucose levels, eliminating the need for blood samples.

NiFe Permanent Magnets for Electric Motors and Turbines

Investigated the properties of NiFe (Nickel-Iron) permanent magnets for potential applications in electric motors and wind turbines.
